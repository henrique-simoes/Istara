## Cross-Session Themes

1. **Notification fatigue** — Across sessions, participants described turning off most notifications due to volume.
2. **Navigation confusion** — Multiple participants struggled to locate features they knew existed, resorting to browser search or help docs.
3. **Performance sensitivity** — Even small delays (2-3 seconds) are perceived as significant blockers.
4. **Onboarding cliff** — Initial setup is praised, but participants describe feeling 'abandoned' after the first week.
